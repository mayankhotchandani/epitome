Product Strategy

A product strategy is the foundation for the entire product lifecycle. As product leaders develop and adjust their product strategy, they zero in on target customers and what they need. This informs the strategic positioning necessary to achieve business success. Once your strategy definition is completed, you can use it to shape your product definition.
